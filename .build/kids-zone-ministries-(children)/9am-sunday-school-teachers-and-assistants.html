9am Sunday School Teachers and Assistants

Sunday School Teachers and Assistants serve every Sunday during the 9:00am service. They lead a small group of elementary students in discipleship and fellowship. Teachers and Assistants are needed for K/5, 1st, 2nd/3rd and 4th-6th Grade classes. Curriculum provided. Time commitment ranges from 6 months to one year. Arrive 15 minutes before service time.
